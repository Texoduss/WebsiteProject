KUROMI

Kuromi is a popular character from the Japanese company Sanrio, known for creating cute, iconic characters like Hello Kitty. Kuromi is depicted as a mischievous, punk-inspired girl with a black jester hat featuring a pink skull on the front. She has a playful yet rebellious personality and is often seen as My Melody's rival, though she has a friendly, somewhat tsundere (hot-and-cold) relationship with her.
